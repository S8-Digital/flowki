AI image tool (Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, Ideogram, etc.) to generate a
logo or app icon for Flowki.

### Visual Style

- **Modern minimalist** — lots of breathing room, no clutter
- **Rounded and soft** — corners default to 14 px radius; nothing sharp or aggressive
- **Warm and welcoming** — feels like home, not a corporate dashboard
- **Clean typography** — Plus Jakarta Sans (geometric-humanist sans-serif)
- **Dual-mode** — works equally well on white and dark backgrounds

## App Icon Prompt (DALL·E / image generation)

> Copy and paste the block below directly into ChatGPT's image generator.

```
Create a modern, minimal app icon for a family organiser app called "Flowki".

Design concept: a smooth, flowing ribbon or wave in the shape of a stylised
letter F, curving fluidly to also suggest connected family members or the
flow of a calm river. The main shape should feel dynamic yet gentle — like
things moving together harmoniously.

Colour: use a gradient from Curious Blue (#2d7ab5) at the top to a slightly
deeper navy blue (#1a4f7a) at the bottom. Optionally add a subtle warm
highlight of Sage Green (#4a9b6f) to one edge of the ribbon.

Canvas: square, 1024 × 1024 px, with a soft white (#ffffff) or very light
blue-gray (#f0f4f8) background. Apply large rounded corners (≈ 22% corner
radius) so it sits naturally as a mobile app icon.

Style: flat with a single subtle depth shadow (not skeuomorphic). Thick
strokes, no thin lines that disappear at small sizes. The icon must be
instantly legible at 32 × 32 px. No text, no letter marks — purely
iconographic.

Do NOT use:
- clip art, cartoon characters, or family silhouettes
- gradients with more than two hues
- dark/heavy backgrounds
- drop shadows larger than the icon itself
```

## Wordmark / Logomark Prompt (DALL·E / image generation)

> Use this when you want the name "Flowki" included in the logo.

```
Design a clean, modern wordmark logo for "Flowki" — a family organiser app
whose tagline is "calm, modern, family-first".

Typography: set "Flowki" in a geometric humanist sans-serif (similar to Plus
Jakarta Sans or Nunito). The "F" should be slightly stylised — its horizontal
bars can taper or curve subtly to suggest fluid movement.

Colour: the word "Flow" in Curious Blue (#2d7ab5), the suffix "ki" in a
slightly lighter tint (#6aadd4), giving a gentle fade effect. Alternatively,
place a small flowing icon (a blue ribbon or wave) to the left of the wordmark.

Background: pure white. The full composition should look great on both white
and dark backgrounds (imagine the lettering inverting to white on dark).

Style: minimal, no decoration, no tagline text in this version, no shadows.
Generous letter-spacing (tracking ≈ 0.04 em). Output as a wide rectangular
asset (e.g. 800 × 200 px).
```

## Design Brief for a Human Designer

If you are working with a human designer, share this brief:

**Flowki** is a calm, family-first organiser app. We need a versatile brand
mark that scales from a 32 × 32 favicon all the way to a full-bleed hero
illustration.

**Core idea:** *flow* — the sense that family life moves smoothly and
effortlessly when the whole household is in sync. The mark should evoke
movement, warmth, and togetherness without resorting to literal family
iconography (no stick figures, no house outlines).

**Deliverables requested:**
1. App icon (1024 × 1024, rounded-corner safe area)
2. Wordmark (SVG, horizontal layout)
3. Favicon (32 × 32 SVG)
4. Dark-mode variants of all three

**Primary colour:** `#2d7ab5` (Curious Blue)  
**Accent palette:** Sage Green `#4a9b6f` · Coral `#e05c47` · Amber `#d4922a` · Lavender `#7c6fc4`  
**Typeface:** Plus Jakarta Sans (already licensed in the app)  
**Mood board references:** Linear, Notion, Cron — minimal SaaS tools with a
warm personality.
